I'm a first year PhD student at the University of Montreal and Mila, supervised by Irina Rish. I'm currently working on a ménage-à-trois of supporting scientific research with language models: mechanistic interpretability to understand how LLMs handle scientific knowledge; inductive biases to improve LLM robustness on scientific domains; and HCI to better understand how LLMs can support scientists in practice. 

When I'm not doing that, I'm likely either fighting futilely against gravity rock climbing, or futilely fighting against friends losing at board games. 

I'm on a quest to find authors that make me feel the way Gene Wolfe did.
